## Description

100% local. 100% offline. 100% yours.

Every copy is raw ore. Foundry Clip casts it into a permanent, searchable archive — your entire clipboard history, your reusable snippets, your recurring boilerplate — forged locally on your Mac and ready the instant you need it again.

Press the hotkey. Search. Paste. The forge never forgets.

**Your clipboard, finally worthy of what you put in it.**
- Unlimited history: Every text, image, file path, and code snippet you've copied, permanently catalogued
- Instant search: Find any past copy in under a second — full text, fuzzy, or by app source
- Snippet library: Save reusable templates, signatures, and boilerplate for one-key paste
- Forge sync: iCloud sync keeps your clipboard history across all your Macs
- Pinned items: Star the copies you reach for constantly — they rise above the slag
- Format-aware paste: Strip rich text formatting when you just want plain text

**The forge casts nothing to the cloud.**
Your clipboard contains everything — passwords typed in the wrong field, draft messages, code with credentials, medical notes, private conversations. Foundry Clip stores all of it on-device, encrypted at rest. Zero telemetry. Zero upload. The history belongs to you, not to a sync server in someone else's datacenter.

**Built for power users who live in copy-paste.**
The developer rotating between terminals, editors, and Slack. The writer drafting in multiple documents. The designer copying hex values and asset paths. The analyst moving data between spreadsheets. Foundry Clip is the forge for everyone who has lost something they copied thirty minutes ago.
